Customer Analysis: Predicting Customer Churn

Customer churn prediction refers to detecting which customers are likely to leave a service or terminate their subscription to a service. It is very critical for businesses because gaining new clients often costs more than retaining the existing ones. Investing in or acquiring new clients is a non-negotiable investment for any company. When a client leaves, it represents a significant loss for the business. A lot of resources need to be channelled into replacing them. In this project, I trained the model and tried to predict the likelihood that a customer will churn.
